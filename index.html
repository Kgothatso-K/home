About Me

I am a data whisperer based in South Africa, with a Google Professional Certificate in Data Analytics. I am skilled in SQL, R, Tableau and Spreadsheets. My educational background is in Mechanical Engineering, Applied Mathematics and Physics.

I believe in the most iconic cliche in the data world, that data driven decision making can be used by society to make better decisions.

This site is designed to showcase case studies and projects while highlighting skills used by data professionals. This site will also serve as a nexus point for both my GitHub and Tableau portfolio sites.
